Goe POS Privacy Policy

Introduction

Goe Point of Sales (GoePOS) is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and delete your personal information when you use our service.

Please read this Privacy Policy thoroughly to ensure that you understand our data processing practices. By registering and/or using our service, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection

In order to access all features and benefits of the Goe Point of Sales app, you are required to create an account in our app and provide a valid email address. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this Privacy Policy.

  • Personal information, such as name, email, phone number, address, and other account details.
  • Device information, such as device ID and device name.
  • Usage information, such as how you use the app and which features you use.

Use of Information

The information that we collect from you may be used for business and/or regulatory purposes, including the following:

  • Registering and identifying you as a user, and verifying, administering, deactivating, and managing your account.
  • Providing, maintaining, developing, enhancing, and improving our services.
  • Responding to your inquiries, requests, and feedback.
  • Processing and facilitating purchase and payment transactions for subscription products.
  • Monitoring and analysing user activity, behaviour, demographic data, trends, and usage of services in our app.
  • Conducting research, analysis, business intelligence, reporting, and service improvement or development.
  • Any other purpose that may be necessary to provide the services that you have opted for.

Deletion of Account and Data

Users have the right to manually delete personal data from the specified features within the application.

If a user wants to terminate their account, including all associated personal data, a formal request must be submitted to our designated contact.

The execution of this request may require up to thirty (30) days, or possibly longer. During this period, the user's account will be deactivated and access will be unavailable.

Security

We protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Although we try our best to provide security in line with industry standards, the internet is not 100% secure or reliable, so we cannot guarantee the complete security of all information sent to us.

You acknowledge that we are not responsible for any intercepted information sent via the internet, and you release us from any and all claims arising out of or related to the use of intercepted information in any unauthorized manner.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. Your continued use of our service following any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
