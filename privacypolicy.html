Goe POS Privacy Policy

1.	Introduction

	Goe Point of Sales (Gö POS) is committed to protecting the privacy of its users.
	This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and delete your personal information when you use our service.
	Please read this Privacy Policy thoroughly to ensure that you understand our data processing practices.
	By registering and/or using our service, you consent to the collection, use, and
	disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2.	Information Collection
	In order to have access to all the features and benefits on Goe Point of Sales App,
	you are required to first create an account on our App and provide us valid email address.
	The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.
	We may collect the following types of information from the Users:
	* Personal information, such as name, email, and phone number, address, etc.
	* Device information, such as device ID and name.
	* Usage information, such as how you use the App and the features you use.

3.	Use of Information

	The information which we collect from you may be utilized for various business and/or regulatory purposes including for
	the following purposes:
	* Register and identify you as a user and to verify, administer, deactivate, and manage your account.
	* Provide, maintain, develop, enhance and improvement of our services.
	* Respond to your inquiries, requests, and feedback.
	* Process and facilitate purchase and payment transactions for the subscription products.
	* Monitor and analyse user activities, behaviour, and demographic data including trends and usage of
	the various services in our App.
	* For purposes of research, analysis, business intelligence, reporting and improvement/development/advancement of
	our services.
	* Any other purpose that may be necessary to provide the services that you have opted for.

4. 	Deletion of Account and Data

	The user possesses the right to manually expunge personal data from the specified functionalities within the Application.

	Should the user desire the termination of their account, encompassing all associated personal data,
	a formal request must be tendered to our designated contact.

	The execution of said request may necessitate a period extending up to thirty (30) days, or possibly longer.
	Throughout this duration, the user's account shall stand in a deactivated status, rendering access thereto unattainable.

5.	Security

6.	Changes to Privacy Policy

	We reserves the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time. Your continued use of our service following any
	changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

7.	Contact Us
